# ROLE_CONFLICT_OF_INTEREST_POLICY.md

## 1. Purpose
This policy defines mandatory rules for identifying, disclosing, managing, and resolving conflicts of interest (COI) within the DAO, its committees, contributors, operators, and affiliated entities. Its objective is to preserve integrity, independence, and trust across governance, clinical, research, and technical decisions.

## 2. Scope
Applies to:
- DAO members and token holders in governance roles
- Committee members (scientific, clinical, ethics, audit, oversight)
- Core contributors and maintainers
- External advisors and contractors
- Any individual with decision-making authority or privileged access
