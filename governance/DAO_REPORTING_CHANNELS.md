# DAO_REPORTING_CHANNELS.md  
**Incident, Ethics & Governance Reporting Framework**

---

## 1. Purpose

This document defines the **mandatory reporting channels** for governance, ethics, safety, and compliance concerns within NeuroGrid.

Its goals are to:
- Enable early detection of risks
- Protect whistleblowers
- Ensure timely intervention
- Maintain regulatory readiness

Reporting is a **protected governance function**, not an act of dissent.

---

## 2. Scope

Applies to reports involving:
- Governance misconduct
- Ethics violations
- Safety failures
- Clinical AI risks
- Compliance breaches
- DAO process manipulation

Across **Core, MedIntel, and DeSci systems**.

---

## 3. Reporting Channel Types

### 3.1 Public Governance Reports  
For non-sensitive governance issues

### 3.2 Confidential Reports  
For ethics, safety, and compliance concerns

### 3.3 Whistleblower Reports  
For serious misconduct or capture risks

### 3.4 Emergency Reports  
For imminent harm or system failure

---

## 4. Submission Requirements

All reports must include:
- Issue classification
- Affected system or process
- Evidence or rationale (where available)
- Urgency level

Anonymous reporting is permitted.

---

## 5. Protection & Non-Retaliation

The following are guaranteed:
- Confidentiality protections
- Anti-retaliation enforcement
- Pseudonymous reporting options
- Sanctions for retaliation

Retaliation constitutes a governance violation.

---

## 6. Review & Triage Process

Reports are:
- Logged immutably
- Triaged by severity
- Routed to appropriate authority
- Time-bound for response

Emergency reports bypass standard queues.

---

## 7. Resolution & Feedback

All reports receive:
- Acknowledgement
- Status updates (where permissible)
- Resolution summary

Sensitive details may be redacted.

---

## 8. Transparency & Metrics

Aggregated reporting metrics include:
- Number of reports by category
- Resolution timelines
- Escalation frequency
- Outcome classifications

Individual identities are never disclosed.

---

## 9. Enforcement

Failure to maintain reporting channels:
- Triggers audits
- Constitutes governance non-compliance
- May invoke emergency oversight

---

## 10. Regulatory Alignment

Aligned with:
- ISO 37002 (Whistleblowing)
- EU AI Act reporting obligations
- FDA adverse event expectations
- OECD accountability standards

---

## 11. Ethical Position

> **Silence is not neutrality. Reporting is protection.**

---

## 12. Binding Status

This document is:
- Mandatory across all NeuroGrid governance layers
- Enforced procedurally and technically
- Subject to external audit
- Amendable only through compliant governance
