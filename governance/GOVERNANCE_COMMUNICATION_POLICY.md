# GOVERNANCE_COMMUNICATION_POLICY.md

Classification: Critical Governance & Compliance Policy
Applies To: NeuroGrid DAO, DeSci Infrastructure, Clinical AI Governance
Version: 1.0
Authority Level: Binding
Review Cycle: Annual or upon governance mechanism change
Custodian: Governance Council in coordination with Ethics & Compliance Committee

---

# 1. Purpose

This policy establishes mandatory rules, channels, controls, and accountability for all governance-related communications within the NeuroGrid DAO.

Its objectives are to:

Ensure clarity, traceability, and auditability of governance communications

Prevent misinformation, governance manipulation, and informal authority signaling

Maintain regulatory-grade records suitable for audits, legal review, and compliance verification

Protect clinical independence and prevent governance pressure on regulated activities

---

# 2. Scope

This policy governs all governance communications, including but not limited to:

Governance proposals and deliberations

Policy announcements and interpretations

Role-based instructions or directives

Emergency notices and compliance advisories

Public-facing governance statements

It applies to all DAO roles, including token holders, councils, committees, operators, and contractors.

---

# 3. Core Communication Principles

All governance communication must adhere to the following principles:

Authority Accuracy
Communications must clearly state the authority under which they are issued.

Non-Coercion
No communication may exert pressure on clinical, scientific, or ethical independence.

Single-Source Validity
Only designated channels constitute official governance communication.

Immutability & Recordability
Governance communications must be logged, timestamped, and preserved.

Regulatory Neutrality
Communications must not misrepresent regulatory status, approvals, or obligations.

---

# 4. Authorized Communication Channels

Only the following channels are recognized as official governance communication mechanisms:

4.1 On-Chain Governance Records

Proposals

Votes

Ratifications

Role assignments

Status: Canonical source of governance truth

4.2 Governance Repository (.md Documentation)

Policies

Frameworks

Formal interpretations

Amendments

Status: Binding reference documents

4.3 Official DAO Bulletins

Scheduled governance updates

Compliance notices

Approved summaries of decisions

Restrictions: Informational only; no new authority creation

4.4 Emergency Governance Notices

Security incidents

Regulatory intervention

System suspension events

Authorization Required:
Governance Council + Ethics & Compliance Committee

---

# 5. Unauthorized Communication

The following are explicitly non-authoritative and may not be used to issue governance direction:

Private messaging platforms

Social media posts

Informal chat channels

Verbal statements or recordings

Unratified community summaries

Use of such channels to imply authority constitutes a governance violation.

---

# 6. Role-Based Communication Authority
6.1 Governance Council

May issue formal governance notices

May interpret existing governance policy

May not issue clinical or technical directives

6.2 Ethics & Compliance Committee

May issue compliance advisories

May suspend communications that violate regulation

Holds corrective authority over misleading statements

6.3 Clinical Oversight Board

May issue clinical governance guidance

Communications are immune to DAO modification

Not subject to token-holder debate

6.4 DAO Operators

May communicate execution status only

No interpretive or policy authority

6.5 Token Holders

May communicate opinions and proposals

Must not represent statements as authoritative unless ratified

---

# 7. Mandatory Communication Disclosures

All official governance communications must include:

Issuing body

Authority basis

Scope of applicability

Effective date

Appeal or review pathway (if applicable)

Failure to include disclosures invalidates the communication.

---

# 8. Misinformation & Correction Protocol

If inaccurate or misleading governance communication is identified:

Immediate flagging by Audit or Compliance function

Temporary suspension of the communication

Issuance of a correction notice

Permanent linkage between original and corrected record

Repeated violations may trigger sanctions.

---

# 9. Communication & Clinical Independence Firewall

No governance communication may:

Direct clinical decisions

Imply mandatory use of AI outputs

Threaten consequences for clinical override

Frame DAO preferences as medical requirements

Violations escalate directly to Compliance and external regulators if required.

---

# 10. Record Retention & Auditability

All governance communications are:

Retained per GOVERNANCE_RECORD_RETENTION_POLICY.md

Indexed for audit retrieval

Protected against retroactive alteration

---

# 11. Enforcement & Sanctions

Violations of this policy may result in:

Communication nullification

Role suspension or revocation

Governance sanctions

Legal or regulatory escalation

---

# 12. Amendments

This policy may only be amended through:

Governance Council proposal

Ethics & Compliance review

DAO ratification

Regulatory compatibility confirmation

---

# 13. Binding Declaration

This policy is binding across all DAO layers.

Any governance action or directive issued outside this policy framework is void ab initio.
